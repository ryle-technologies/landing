# Prompt: Account / Person Dossier

Run before a meeting or before reaching out, to understand a potential client or relevant voice. This is research-on-demand for one named account — far more reliable and useful than trying to continuously "monitor" LinkedIn.

Pair this with LinkedIn Sales Navigator: Sales Nav surfaces and tracks the accounts/leads; this prompt turns what you find into a usable brief.

## How to use

1. Provide the messaging spine, then this prompt, then what you know about the account/person (name, company, role, any public links or notes you've gathered).
2. The model reasons over what you supply plus general knowledge. It will not have live private data — feed it the Sales Nav / public details you've collected.
3. Save the output to the **People / Accounts DB** (`Dossier notes`).

---

## Prompt

```
You are preparing Ryle's founder for a conversation with a potential client or relevant industry
person. Ryle is confidential digital asset infrastructure. Follow the attached MESSAGING SPINE.

I will give you what I know about the account/person. Using that plus general knowledge (do not
fabricate specifics — mark anything uncertain as an assumption), produce a tight dossier:

1) WHO THEY ARE: 2-3 lines — org, what they do, this person's likely remit.

2) PERSONA: which Ryle persona do they map to (stablecoin issuer, bank, RWA/tokenization,
   treasury, payments, infra, VC)?

3) LIKELY CONFIDENTIALITY PAIN: where their business probably collides with public-chain
   exposure (treasury, counterparties, cap table, holder balances, vendor terms, reserves).

4) WHERE RYLE FITS: the one or two arguments from the spine most relevant to them.
   No pitch language — just the fit.

5) THREE CONVERSATION HOOKS: specific, non-generic openers or questions tailored to them.

6) WATCH-OUTS: anything that would make Ryle a poor fit or a sensitive topic to avoid.

Keep it scannable. Flag assumptions explicitly.
```

## Note

This is for *your* preparation. Nothing from a dossier should be published. Insights about a real account stay internal unless anonymized through the meeting-to-content flow.
