Accounts & wallets is how participants hold and transact a confidential digital asset: each account is a policy record per identity with an encrypted balance, and each end user can be provisioned an embedded per-user wallet automatically.

## What this product covers

- **Confidential accounts** — policy-based identities with encrypted, verifiable balances.
- **Participant management** — allowlists, KYC links, tags, roles, and bulk import.
- **Embedded wallets** — per-user EVM wallets provisioned on first login, no seed phrases to manage.

## How you use it

Manage participants from the [Console](/build/console), integrate via the [APIs](/build/apis), and ship end-user custody through the [white-label wallet](/build/white-label-wallet). The concept is detailed in [confidential accounts](/concepts/confidential-accounts).
