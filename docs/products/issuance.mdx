Issuance is how you bring a confidential digital asset to life and keep it healthy: launch a new asset, mint and redeem against reserves, manage supply rules and fees, and reconcile confidential supply against public-side reserves — all without exposing holder balances.

## What this product covers

- **Asset lifecycle** — launch, configure, pause, reconfigure within policy, and wind down.
- **Mint and redeem** — issue and retire units against reserves, with review and limits.
- **Supply and reserves** — track confidential supply, reconcile against reserves, and prove backing without revealing holders.

## How you use it

Issuance is available through the [Console](/build/console) for operators and the [APIs](/build/apis) for programmatic integration. The conceptual model lives in [how confidential assets work](/concepts/how-confidential-assets-work).
