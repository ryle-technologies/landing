Confidential payments is the private transfer rail: accounts send and receive a confidential digital asset on public-chain settlement, while amounts and counterparties stay encrypted and the network still verifies every transfer is valid.

## What this product covers

- **Confidential transfers** — encrypted amounts and parties, verifiable without disclosure.
- **Settlement** — runs on EVM-compatible chains; the chain target is a configuration choice. See [supported networks](/reference/supported-networks).
- **Limits and policy** — per-account and per-asset rules govern who can transact and how much.

## How you use it

Confidential payments is exposed through the [APIs](/build/apis) for integration, operated from the [Console](/build/console), and can be surfaced to end users via the [white-label wallet](/build/white-label-wallet). See applied scenarios in [use cases](/use-cases/b2b-settlement).
