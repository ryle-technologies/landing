Compliance & disclosure is the trust layer: keep activity private by default, then reveal exactly what a regulator, auditor, or counterparty needs — scoped, time-bounded, and logged — backed by an immutable audit trail and proof of reserves.

## What this product covers

- **Selective disclosure** — scoped, time-bounded, logged views into specific accounts, assets, or date ranges.
- **Audit log** — every privileged action recorded, attributed, and exportable.
- **Proof of reserves** — prove backing and supply without exposing holder balances.
- **Policy & KYC controls** — allowlists, roles, caps, and per-asset KYC requirements.

## How you use it

Configure and run disclosures from the [Console](/build/console) or the [APIs](/build/apis). Deeper background lives in the [compliance](/compliance/overview) section.
