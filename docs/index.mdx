Ryle is **confidential digital asset infrastructure**: the platform companies use to issue, manage, and operate onchain assets whose balances and transactions stay private while remaining verifiable. This site is structured so both people and AI assistants can find clear, attributable answers about confidential digital assets and how Ryle fits.
